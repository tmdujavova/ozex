COMPANY PROFILE

OZEX Co. Ltd. Prešov was established in 1992. Since its formation, company has undergone a dynamic development. At the beginning, company was focusing mainly on the clothing industry, but later it has diversified majority of its activities into the field of travel and tourism. Of the original activities, the company has retained the ownership in the joint venture - Egotex Ltd., which is Slovak-Italian production company focusing on the production of the luxury men’s clothing for world known designer brands CORNELIANI and RALPH LAUREN.

Other industrial activities related to the garment industry, which were founded and built by OZEX Ltd. in Bulgaria and Ukraine were successfully sold. Both production plants are still active and new owners are producing brand products sold throughout the Europe.

Beginning of the diversification into the new segment of travel and tourism started in 2005, when the foundations of the new luxury complex PLAZA BEACH RESORT were laid. This complex has consistently developed into the unique and exceptional resort of its kind not only in Presov or Slovakia, but also across the central European region – Poland, Hungary and Ukraine.

Unique complex PLAZA BEACH RESORT consists of luxury Boutique hotel VILLA PLAZA & SPA**** with the stylish restaurant and congress hall. Moreover, there are open air summer pools PLAZA BEACH with complex equipment for the use of leisure activities not only for hotel guests, but also for the public.

Business portfolio of the OZEX Ltd. has expanded in 2017, when new project PLAZA RESIDENCE started, with the mission to develop the nearby area. Company has built the residential complex, that consist of 4 apartment buildings with the 24 flats and 4 penthouses.

The strategy of OZEX Ltd. Prešov is to create philosophy of sustainability in business mobility and sharp competitive environment, which emphasizes on the quality of service and reliability for our clients.
